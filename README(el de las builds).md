Andro Dunos v.0.5

github: https://github.com/ofaura/Andro_Dunos

Controls:

F1 - To see colliison boxes
F2 - Autowin
F3 - Aulose
F5 - GodMode

PLAYER 1:

	W - Move up.

	A - Move left.

	S - Move down.

	D - Move right.

	SPACE - Shoot.
	
	E - Change weapon type.

PLAYER 2:

	UP - Move up.

	LEFT - Move left.

	DOWN - Move down.

	RIGHT - Move right.

	RIGHT CONTROL - Shoot.
	
	RIGHT SHIFT - Change weapon type.


ENTER - Go to next stage.

ESC / Click in the red cross - Close application.
